Battle Sites of the United States

This is a map of potentially all known Battle Sites, Battlefields, or conflicts in the United States and some just outside the U.S. border (i.e. across the border in Mexico or Canada). It is an attempt to recreate and improve upon a 1967 Battle Sites Map compiled by the USGS. This will be a "living" document and will be updated as information is gathered.

The 1967 map did not contain all battle site or battlefields. Every effort will be made to include each battle site from each war period. In order to see the battle sites in Alaska and the Hawaiian Islands, you will need to zoom out and pan to the west. While most battle sites and battlefields are protected and a part of the National Park Service or some State or Local entity. There are many sites that are not protected and in fact considered endangered. Many sites cannot be located with 100% certainty due to overdevelopment. This map could potentially aid historical societies and protection groups in the preservation of some sites.

Data Sources

Initial battle site locations are based upon 1967 Battle Sites Map.

Each site was looked up via the internet. Many have one or more sites that have information about the battle. (i.e. Wiki, National Park Service, State Parks, etc.)
Latitude and Longitude site locations were researched and confirmed or approximated via Google Maps with the information derived from websites that contain information about the battle sites.
Since the data could not be obtained from the USGS, it was compiled in an excel spreadsheet, then saved as an .csv file for use.
